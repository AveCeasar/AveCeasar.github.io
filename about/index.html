Why This Exists

ChartVault is the public chart layer behind Cezary's market workflow.

The goal is simple: put the useful market views in one place, explain them fast, and make it easier to move from raw charts to an actual tape read. Breadth, concentration, sector rotation, drawdowns, volatility, macro ratios, and yield-curve signals belong together if they help people understand the tape faster.

What You Will Find

Market internals, sector leadership, volatility, drawdowns, macro relationships, and ratio charts that usually take too much digging to assemble cleanly.

How To Use It

Start with the question you want answered, use the tag flow or your saved charts, then read the short interpretation notes on each page before moving to the related charts.

Update Rhythm

Charts refresh on a daily cadence. That is deliberate: the focus is regime shifts, participation changes, and tactical market reads, not intraday noise.

Who Makes It

ChartVault is built by Cezary. It exists because too many useful market checks are buried across terminals, screenshots, and half-explained dashboards. The site is meant to be the clean, reusable front-end for those checks.

Why Trust It

The site is intentionally narrow: static daily charts, plain-language reading guides, and locally derived datasets where possible. There is no promise of prediction-by-dashboard here. The value is in making the signal set legible, comparable, and easy to revisit.
