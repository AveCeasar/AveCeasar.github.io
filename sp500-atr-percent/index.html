How To Read It

  • Volatility spikes show stress, but what matters most is whether volatility stays high or quickly mean-reverts.
  • Rising volatility with flat prices usually signals a more fragile tape than the index headline suggests.
  • Compare current levels with prior stress zones rather than treating every jump as equally important.
