Methodology

  • S&P 500 drawdown is measured as the percent drop from the highest close seen since the shared local start date.
  • The VIX line uses the raw daily close so volatility spikes stay legible next to equity stress.
  • Both lines currently begin on 2021-03-11, the first shared trading date in the local S&P 500 and VIX files.
