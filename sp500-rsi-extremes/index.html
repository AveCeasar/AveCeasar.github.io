How To Read It

  • Rising breadth means more stocks are participating; falling breadth means leadership is narrowing.
  • If the index is strong while breadth weakens, concentration is likely doing more of the work.
  • Extremes matter most when they reverse, because that is when internals begin to confirm or reject the headline move.
